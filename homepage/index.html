Biography of Shrek

Shrek Video

Hi, I'm Shrek! I'm a fictional ogre character created by William Steig. I'm the main protagonist of the DreamWorks animated film series of the same name.

I live in a swamp, but I've found true happiness after meeting and marrying Princess Fiona. You might know me for my sarcasm, humor, and my layers, much like an onion.

Despite my grumpy exterior, I like to believe that I have a heart of gold, and I've somehow managed to endear myself to audiences worldwide.
